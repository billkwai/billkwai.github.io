What Drives Me

Execution. Execution. Execution.

I've always had an infectious enthusiasm for technology, and chasing my passion has put me on a path where I'm always searching for new experiences. Studying computer science has never been a means to an end, but rather a way to satisfy my intellectual curiosity and understand the core of the technology ecosystem. Along the way, I've realized that writing code isn't the only way to make an impact. My skills are most effective when I can guide a technology product from vision and execution to completion. I find it extremely rewarding to coordinate between stakeholders, designers, and engineers to bring my visions to life.

Behind the Scenes
