Camp Kesem

Cancer Sucks. Kesem Helps.

Camp Kesem is a summer camp for kids whose parents have been affected by cancer. For the past two summers, I've been involved as a camp counselor at Camp Kesem. We spend the school year fundraising and we put on this camp, free of charge to families, during the first week of summer. Every single one of these kids are amazing and learning what hardships they've endured has been the most inspiring experience I've ever had.

Outdoors

Showering is for losers.


I remember hating my first sleep-away camp in the 6th grade so much that I cried. It wasn't until college that I started loving the outdoors. When you spend most of your days around stressed out college students and a never-ending stream of messages, spending a few days outside is an amazing change of pace. Bonus points for clear skies and late night stargazing.
